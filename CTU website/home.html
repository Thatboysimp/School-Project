Welcome to CTU-Buddy!

Greetings from CTU-Buddy, the best study partner available to City Technical University students. With its user-friendly platform, CTU-Buddy aims to improve your educational experience by facilitating easy timetable management, peer engagement, resource sharing, and academic community engagement.
