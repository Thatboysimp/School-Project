Welcome to the CTU-Buddy Discussion Page! Here you can meet and interact with other City Technical University students for meaningful conversations. Key features include threaded discussions, user profiles, and moderation tools to ensure respectful and productive discussions.

Highlighted Key Features:

  • Interactive Conversations: Discuss important issues with classmates.
  • Threaded Discussions: Organized communication for easy reading.
  • User Profiles: Connect with students who share similar study topics.
  • Moderation Tools: Maintain a respectful environment.
